Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Cascade.

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter your habit name and optional description, then tap "Save".

How do streaks work?

A streak counts consecutive days you've completed a habit. Miss a day and it resets, but your best streak is saved.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Info tab → scroll down → tap "Restore Purchases". Use the same Apple ID as your original purchase.

My habits aren't syncing

Ensure you're signed in with the same account on both devices and have an internet connection. Try signing out and back in.

How do I delete a habit?

Swipe left on any habit to reveal the delete option. You can also archive habits to keep your history.

What's included in Cascade Pro?

Unlimited habits, AI coach, premium themes, advanced statistics, notes & journaling, and data export. One-time $4.99 purchase.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed through Apple at reportaproblem.apple.com.

How do I delete my account?

Email us at cascadehabits@gmail.com with subject "Account Deletion Request".
