Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new habit?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, enter your habit name and optional description, then tap "Save". Your habit will appear in your list immediately.

How do streaks work?

A streak counts consecutive days you've completed a habit. Complete your habit each day to keep your streak going. If you miss a day, your current streak resets to zero, but your best streak is saved.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to the Info tab (ℹ️), scroll down, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.

My habits aren't syncing between devices

Make sure you're signed in with the same account on both devices. Check that you have an active internet connection. Try signing out and back in to refresh the sync.

How do I delete a habit?

Swipe left on any habit in your list to reveal the delete option. You can also archive habits instead of deleting them if you want to keep your history.

What's included in Cascade Pro?

Cascade Pro unlocks unlimited habits (free version allows 3), premium themes, advanced statistics, custom categories, notes & journaling, smart reminders, and data export. It's a one-time purchase of $4.99 - pay once, yours forever!

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are processed through Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find your Cascade purchase, and request a refund.

How do I delete my account and data?

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at cascadehabits@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We'll process your request within 30 days.
